09 // questions
Are the P&Ls actually real? Whose books open?

The brands we fully own and control open completely, every month, every line, including net profit, including red months. Brands with outside partners open to the extent the partners agree, and we tell you which is which up front. What we will never do is post a "revenue screenshot" with the costs cropped out. If a number is on the P&L, it's the real one.

Why would you show everything? What's the catch?

Three reasons. One: transparency is the only thing the guru industry can't copy, because their numbers don't survive daylight. Two: building in public makes our own operation sharper; 50 operators watching is the best accountability money can buy. Three: the best founders in this region end up knowing each other, and we'd rather they meet in our room. We're building these brands either way. The network just opens the door while we do it.

I'm pre-launch. Should I join?

If you're seriously building, yes, this is the cheapest education in the market: you'll watch product selection, supplier negotiation, pricing and launch happen live before you spend your own money on those mistakes. If you're collecting ideas with no intention of launching, save your AED 499.

What happens on the two weekly calls?

The Growth Deep Dive reviews member businesses: your ad accounts, creatives, landing pages, retention, offers, live on screen. The Operator Session opens ours: what we launched, tested, scaled and fixed that week. Both recorded, library included. Bring numbers, not opinions.

Is this UAE-only?

The systems are GCC-wide and most translate anywhere, but the texture is deliberately local: White Friday not Black Friday, Ramadan pacing, COD and RTO management, Tabby and Tamara, Noon and Amazon.ae. The in-person events are UAE-based. If you operate elsewhere in the GCC, everything except the venue still applies.

Are the in-person events included in the AED 499?

No, and that's deliberate. Events are ticketed separately and open to members only. A paid door is the only filter that reliably fills a room with people who run real businesses instead of people collecting selfies. Venues, food and small rooms cost real money, and we'd rather charge the attendees than bury it in everyone's membership. Founding members get founding pricing on every event, forever.

Can I really cancel anytime?

Yes. Month to month, cancel in two clicks. And inside the first 14 days, one message gets a full refund. A membership built on open P&Ls can't have a fine-print exit. The door works both ways or the transparency means nothing.
